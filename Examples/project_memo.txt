**Research topic, questions, and objectives:**
The project focuses on documenting and examining the living conditions of women in Ireland during World War II and the influence of the war on their private and public roles.

**Methodology:**
Biographical interviews

**Participants and data collected:**
Interviews with around 30 women from different familial and social backgrounds. These interviews were conducted between 1995 and 2006, during which the women reflected on their lives between 1939 and 1945.
